Metaphysics

Planes of Existence

At a fundamental level, existence is nothing more than the movement of darill acting under their own rules. As seen in the medium scale section of the particle physics chapter, though, it can still be useful to abstract this movement, and develop models to explain it.

In this model, each body is emergent from the one below: a sufficiently complex structure on one level gives rise to phenomena on a higher level that are greater than the sum of their parts. For a lifeform, a body is associated with each plane.

Fifth Plane

The fifth plane, also known as the “energy plane”, or the “fifth plane, which binds”, is the realm in which darill form quasi‑particles. A body on this level is called a Self.
