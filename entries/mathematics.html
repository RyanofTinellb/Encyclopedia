Mathematics

High Lulani uses balanced sesquidecimal. The use of a balanced base can half the length of mantissae, with (round brackets) signifying repeated digits, and [square brackets] signifying mirrored digits.

For example:

  • ½ = 0.(7)
  • ⅓ = 0.5
  • ¼ = 0.[4]
  • ⅕ = 0.3
  • ⅙ = 0.2(7)
  • 71 = 0.(2)
  • 171 = 0.[2]
  • 161 = 0.25
  • 151 = 0.1(7)
